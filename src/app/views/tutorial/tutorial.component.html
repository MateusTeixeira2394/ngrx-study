To win the game you must step in all grounds without the mine. In a few words, if you step in any ground with the mine, you'll lose. But don't worry, all trodden ground will give you any tip where the mine is.

This kind of ground say the quantity of mine around it. The example above means that has exactly 2 mines around it.

Grounds like these are called safety zone, that means that all grounds around it don't have any mine.

If you are certain that a ground has a mine, you can use a flag to help you see the game better and not have the risk to step on the mine. The example above shows one mine around the ground with number 1. Thus, we are sure that the unknown ground has a mine. So, we flag the ground so that we don't have the probability to step it.
